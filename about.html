About us

About us

Homestead Healthcare Services, Inc. exists for one simple yet powerful reason — to serve people with dignity, compassion, and purpose. At the heart of our organization is a genuine commitment to individuals with developmental and intellectual disabilities who deserve not only quality care, but meaningful lives filled with opportunity, independence, and belonging. As a DDA Group Home Residential, Personal Support, and Community Developmental Services provider, Homestead Healthcare Services was founded on the belief that every person carries potential, value, and a story worth honoring. Our role is not merely to provide services — it is to walk alongside the individuals we support as partners in their growth, independence, and well-being. Our homes are more than residences. They are safe, nurturing environments where individuals are respected, encouraged, and empowered to thrive. Within every program we offer, we focus on building confidence, strengthening daily living skills, and creating opportunities for active community participation. Homestead Healthcare Services approaches care with what we proudly call a heart of gold. This means listening before acting, understanding before planning, and supporting each individual in a way that reflects who they truly are. Families trust us because we treat their loved ones as part of our extended family. Our professional team includes compassionate caregivers, trained support specialists, and experienced coordinators who share a common mission — improving lives every day. We combine structured care with warmth, professionalism with empathy, and accountability with genuine human connection. Through our residential services, personal support programs, and community developmental initiatives, we help individuals build lives of dignity, inclusion, and possibility. Every smile, every milestone, and every step toward independence reminds us why our work matters. Homestead Healthcare Services is not just about healthcare. It is about people, purpose, and the power of compassionate support.
